sama - together (mutual, adj)
makasama

usap - converse?(mutual, verb) discussion? (noun)
kausap - productive stem (kausapin, makausap - both object focus, magkausap - plural focus)
mag-usap - plural focus

bigo - failure or disappointment (noun)

takot - fear (noun)
matakot - active: to be scared, object: to scare someone
ikatakot        to cause fear (active focus) "ang ikinatakot ko.."

iwan - leave
maiwan, iwanan - active: to be left,  object: to leave something
Naiwan ko ang ID ko.	I left my ID behind.
Iniwanan ng mga suspek ang dalawang biktima sa isang parking lot.

tapos - finish(ed)? tapos - end (noun), tapós - finished (adj)
matapos - active: to be finished/over, obj: to finish/complete something
makatapos - active: to finish/complete, obj: to be finished/completed
magtapós, makapagtapós - to graduate
Hindi pa rin natapos ang digmaan.	Still, the war was not over.
Hindî ko natapos ang pelíkulá kasí nákatulog akó.	I was not able to finish the movie because I fell asleep.
Hindi tayo makakatapos ng isang pelikula.

hulog - fall (verb)
mahulog - active: fall/be dropped, obj: 'to drop something'
Nahulog akó.	I fell.
Nahulog ang pinggan ng kapatid kong babae sa sahig.	My sister dropped her plate on the floor.

sisi - blame or regret
masisi - active: to be blamed, obj: to blame someone
Hindi niya masisisi si Homer sa sinabi nito sa kaniya.	Homer could not blame him for what he said.
Si Jim ang nasisi sa pagkawalâ ng libró ni Jane.	Jim was the one blamed for the loss of Jane's book.
