Definition for the Tagalog word makakita:
makakita
[verb] to happen to see; to see; to see one or some or many; to be able to see; to catch sight of; to be able to find

 Usage Notes:
Use "makakita" (Actor focus) to mean "to see one or some of many," or to say you saw a non-specific thing.
Use a different verb, "makita" (Object focus), to mean "to see a specific thing."
