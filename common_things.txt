When I tell you to (have told you to) shut up, you shut up.	Kapág sinabihan kitáng tumahimik (ka), tumahimik ka.
Please tell Jack to come here.	Pakisabihan mo si Jack na pumuntá dito.
Where did you tell him to sit (set him)?	Saán mo siyá pinaupô?
Keep going until I tell you (as long as I have not told you) to stop.	Sige lang hanggang hindi ko pa sinasabi na tumigil ka.
I hope to see you later.	Sana makita kita mamaya.
Until later.	Hanggang mamaya.
Get well soon.	Magpagaling ka kaagad.
I want to know.	Gusto kong malaman.
You can tell me.	Maaari mong sabihin sa akin.
I will tell you.	Sasabihin ko sa iyo.
You impressed me.	Pinahanga mo ako.
I like what you did.	Nagustuhan ko ang iyong ginawa.
We have the same attitude.	Pareho tayo ng ugali.
Where do you want to go?	Saan mo gustong pumunta?
You surprised me.	Ginulat mo ako.
I want us to go on a trip.	Gusto ko na magbiyahe tayo.
I am happy to know it.	Masaya akong malaman it.
