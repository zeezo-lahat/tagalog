pag-an: to do something WITH SOMEONE/SOMETHING (reciprocal action on object) - pinag-an pinagCV-an pagCV-an

* pag-usapan converse with someone about ANG - object focus
         usap     pinag-usapan    pinapag-usapan    papag-usapan      pag-usapan
the actor is ng and generally a plural such as natin, mamin, ng mga lalaki.

* pagtakpán - cover up / whitewash - locative focus
         takíp    pinagtakpán     pinagtátakpán     pagtátakpán       pagtakpán
takíp - a cover, lid, cap

contrast with 
takpan - to cover something, object focused "takpan mo ang mga mata mo -> cover your eyes"
itakíp - to use something to cover / as a cover, object focused
  itinátakíp ang kumot sa kaniyáng ulo -> used the blanket to cover her head
magtakíp - to cover, actor focused
  magtakíp ka ng bibíg -> cover your mouth
umakíp - to cover, to block, actor focus
matakpán - to hide, to block, ? focus

ipag- (ng) does something FOR someone/something else (ang) (ex: ipaglaban) (*like i- but with mag- verbs) (benefactor)
ipag- (ng) does something REGARDING (to, with, etc.) a thing (ang) (object)
to enjoy ANG           saya     pinagsaya       pinagsasaya       ipapagsaya        ipapagsaya      ipag
brag (about/to ANG??)  yabang   ipinag-yabang   ipinapag-yabang   ipag-yayabang     ipag-yabang     ipag
win something(?)         wagi'    ipinagwagi'     ipinapagwagi'     ipapagwagi'       ipagwagi'       ipag
be puzzled by ANG?     taka     ipinagtaka      ipinagtataka      ipagtataka        ipagtaka        ipag
      
- All are object focus
