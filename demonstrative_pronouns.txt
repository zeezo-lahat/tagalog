Demonstrative pronouns: (of) this/that, here/there

this/that  | (of) this/that  | here/there  |   here/there      |  here/there
 (ang)     |      (ng)       |   (sa)      |    (nasa)         | (existential)
 itO       |      nitO       | dito/rito   |  nandito/nArito   |     hEto    
 iyAn      |      niyAn      | diyAn/riyAn |  nandiyAn/nAriyan |     ayAn    
 iyOn      |      noOn       | doOn/roOn   |  nandoOn/nAroon   |     ayOn    

Note the first letter of ALL the ang (i), ng (n), sa (d/r), and nasa (n) demonstrative pronouns.

re nasa pronouns: nandiyan is often nandyan or andyan.  others maybe also...
