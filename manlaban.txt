Manlálabán akó kung kailangan!	I will fight if need be!
Nanlálabán siyá nang buóng lakás.	She fights/resists with all her might.
Huwág manlabán kung may baríl ang mágnanakaw.	Don't resist if the thief has a gun.
Nanlálabán nang hustó si Mina sa lalaki.	Mina fights hard against the man.
Napahamak siyá nang manlabán siyá.	She was injured when she resisted.
Manlabán kung sa palagáy mo ay tamà ka.	Defend yourself if you think you are right.
Sana ay mas nanlabán pa akó sa kaniyá.	I should have tried harder to fight him off.
Bukód diyán, nanlabán din si Gail.	Besides that, Gail also fought back.
Hindî nanlabán si Rina kayâ hindî siyá nasaktán.	Rina didn't fight back so she wasn't hurt.
Patuloy na nanlálabán ang mga manggagawà.	The workers keep on resisting.
Manganganib ka kung manlálabán ka.	You will be in danger if you will fight back.
Manlálabán ka ba kung ninanakawan ka?	Would you fight if you were being robbed?
Nanlabán ang mágnanakaw nang mahuli siyá ng mga pulís.	The robber struggled with the policemen when they caught him.
