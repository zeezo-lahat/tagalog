Definition for the Tagalog word nga:

   ngâ  [34]Play audio #2407 [35]Save to Flash Cards
   [enclitic]  indeed; yes indeed; truly; please (sentence tone softener);
   really; actually; definitely; of course; as expected; haven't you?
   [36]Root: nga
