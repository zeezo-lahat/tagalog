Tulóy ka!	Come in! (Informal)
Tulóy pô kayó!	Come in! (polite)
Nágising tulóy ang batà.	As a result, the child woke up.
Magdilíg ka na tulóy ng halaman.	You might as well water the plants.
Ináabuso mo kasí ang pagkain ng matatamís kayâ nagkasakít ka tulóy.	Because you overindulge in sweets that's why you eventually got sick.
Tuloy ka nang wala ako.	Go on without me.
