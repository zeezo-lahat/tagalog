dấu - ("yuh-ooh") tones:
flat tone: level, but somewhat high
sắc: up tone - raise your chin, finish with a throat close.  "ya know??" sound
huyền: down - lower your chin, look down, let air out of your lungs, like saying
       "ahhh" to the doctor.  downer/disappointed sound
hỏi: question mark - dip, down-up, like asking question.  finish strong up
ngã: wavy - in south, same as hỏi.  (in north, broken in middle like "uh oh" and higher)
nặng: dot - chop off any letters that come after vowel and flex your abs
      dip/dot - go down quickly, then hit a final distinct tone after a slight
      pause.  shape, but don't pronounce the consanants after it.

Note: up and dot tones will become sharp and short when the syllable ends in p, t, or c.
