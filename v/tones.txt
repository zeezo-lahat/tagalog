dấu - ("yuh-ooh") tones
flat tone: level, but somewhat high
sắc: up tone - raise your chin.  "ya know??" sound
huyền: down tone - lower your chin, look down.  downer/bummer/disappointed sound
hỏi: question mark - dip, down-up
ngã: wavy/tilde - broken in middle like "uh oh" 
nặng: dot - chop off any letters that come after vowel and flex your abs
      dip/dot - go down quickly, then hit a final distinct tone after a slight pause
      shape, but don't pronounce the consanants after it.

1. Sắc (up tone)
2. Huyền (Low down tone)
3. Hỏi (question tone)
4. Ngã (wave tone)
5. Nặng (dot tone)
