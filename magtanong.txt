magtanóng  | itanóng  | matanóng  | maitanóng  | makapagtanóng  | ipagtanóng
Maaarì pô bang magtanóng?	Can I ask something? (polite)
Maaarì ba akóng magtanóng?	May/Can I ask a question? (more formal)
Nagtanóng akó sa iyó kung kailán siyá daratíng.	I asked you when he was coming.
Magtanóng ka lang.	Feel free to ask (a question).
Pwede ba akóng magtanóng?	Can/May I ask a question?
May magtátanóng pa ba?	Does anyone here still have a question to ask?
Kanino puwedeng magtanóng dito kung anóng oras daratíng ang trén?	Who can I ask here as to what time the train will arrive?
Sino ang nagtátanóng?	Who's asking?
Huwág kang mahiyâng magtanóng.	Don't be shy to ask (a question).
Maraming nagtátanóng kay John kung may asawa na siyá.	Many have been asking John if he's already married.
Aber nga, magtatanong ka na naman?	Dare you ask me another question?
