Kompletohin ang mga pangungusap gamit ang wastong markers (ang, si, sina)

Complete the sentences with the correct markers (ANG, SI, SINA).

Abogado _ang_ babae.  The woman is a lawyer.
Estudyante _si_ Rita sa UP.  Rita is a student at UP.
Gusto ko _ang_ mangga.  I like the mango.
Kailangan ko _ang_ libro.  I need the book.
Nag-aaral _sina_ Fely.  Fely and company are studying.
Natulog _ang_ bata sa kwarto.  The child slept in the room.
_Sina_ Martin at Mark ay nag-aaral.  Martin and Mark are studying.
Estudyante _sina_ Rita at Fe sa UP.  Rita and Fe are students at UP.
Nasa Maynila _sina_ Lorna.  Lorna and company are in Manila.
May dalawang kotse _si_ Dolly.  Dolly has two cars
Nasa mesa _ang_ kape.  The coffee is on the table.
Mabait _si_ Lito.  Lito is nice.
May isang kapatid _ang_ lalaki.  The man has a brother.
_Si_ Jenny ang kumuha ng mga papel.  Jenny was the one who took the papers.
_Ang mga_ tao ay nasa simbahan.  The people are in the church.
